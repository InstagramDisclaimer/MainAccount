1) Status & identity

I was born a Canadian citizen and have held only Canadian citizenship since birth. I hold no other passports and have never held any other nationality.

I am a private citizen of Canada. My place of residence and employment is the United Arab Emirates (UAE).

2) Not an influencer / not for sale

I am not an influencer and I have no desire to ever become one. I do not seek sponsorships, clout, brand deals, paid narratives, or any form of paid “influence.” I am content with my financial situation and I do not care about any additional money offered to me. No amount of money “thrown at me” will ever buy my voice, my opinions, my image, or my behavior. I will never sell out.

Separately and explicitly: I will only ever work for myself and my family. I have never and will never call another human being “boss”, no exceptions ever.

3) Personal capacity / not representation

All content and activity on any of my social accounts is shared strictly in a personal capacity. It is not made on behalf of, or as a representative for, any employer, client, partner, brand, organization, or any person connected to me.

Nothing on my pages should be interpreted as official statements, organizational positions, or institutional advocacy.

4) Non-endorsement rule (all engagement)

Any likes, reactions, follows, comments, saves, bookmarks, reposts, retweets, reshares, quote-shares, story shares, tags, mentions, clicks, views, or engagement of any kind are NOT endorsements.

Engagement does not indicate agreement, support, approval, sponsorship, alignment, or adoption of any message, theme, claim or viewpoint expressed by the original content, creator, or any linked material.

  • Engagement may occur for many reasons: documentation, awareness, critique, discussion, media literacy, research, or algorithmic discovery.
  • Engagement may also be accidental (mis-taps) or incidental due to platform UI/algorithm behavior.

5) Political content: explicit non-endorsement

Any engagement with political content (including parties, politicians, governments, policies, elections, geopolitics, conflicts, activism, ideologies or public affairs) is strictly non-endorsement and does not reflect my views.

It must not be interpreted as support for any political position, group, movement, ideology, public figure, or cause. This also applies to content that could be interpreted as political commentary, satire, criticism, or advocacy.

Nothing on my pages represents the views or endorsements of anyone associated with me, including (without limitation) family, friends, colleagues, clients, employers, contractors, business partners, or any related entities.

6) Third-party content, reposts, links

Any reposted/shared content is third-party content attributable to its original source/author. Sharing does not imply I have verified it, agree with it, or endorse it.

External links, screenshots, clips, embedded posts, and quoted material are provided for reference/context and remain the responsibility of the original publisher.

7) Accuracy, rumors, verification

I am not a news outlet. I do not claim that shared content is accurate, complete, current, or properly contextualized.

I do not intend to circulate false, misleading, or unverified information. Readers should rely on official sources for confirmation.

If you believe something posted or shared is incorrect, misleading, or lacking context, contact me and I will review it for correction or removal where appropriate.

8) No intent to insult, defame, harass

No post, share, or engagement is intended to defame any person, harm anyone’s reputation, harass, threaten, intimidate, or provoke hostility. I do not encourage pile-ons, targeting, doxxing, or harassment of any kind.

Nothing on my pages is intended to disrespect religions, cultures, communities, or protected symbols, and I do not promote hate, extremism, violence, or unlawful conduct.

9) No affiliation / sponsorship

Tags, mentions, and links do not imply partnership, endorsement, affiliation, sponsorship, or representation unless explicitly stated in a written agreement.

No content should be construed as advertising, solicitation, or promotion unless explicitly labeled as such.

10) Legacy activity & evolving views

Social media platforms may display years of historical engagement. Any legacy interactions that may appear distasteful, outdated, humorous, satirical, or misinterpretable should not be assumed to reflect my current beliefs, intentions, or values.

Where any past engagement exists, it should be interpreted under the same rule stated above: engagement is not endorsement.

11) Removal / rights holder requests

If you are a rights holder of content posted here, or if you believe content misrepresents you or could cause harm, contact me and I will review promptly for removal, clarification, or credit as appropriate.
